🤝Project Collaboration Guidelines - 💡Before Contacting Us

To save time and effort for both parties, and to quickly clarify your requirements for providing reasonable quotes and timeframes, we hope you can prepare a requirements document outlining your application needs before contacting us.

The process of writing a requirements document will help you review your project, clarify your project expectations, and make it easier to “compare options” with a clear requirements document, reducing misunderstandings that might arise from ambiguous requirements during communication.

If you don’t know how to write a requirements document, here are some examples (not mandatory, for reference only):

  • Project background and objectives: Introduce the origin of the project, the problems to be solved, and the overall goals

  • Functional requirements: List the features the system needs to implement, including user interface/role permissions/processing logic, etc.

  • Non-functional requirements: List the system’s performance metrics/security requirements/availability/reliability/usability and other non-functional requirements

  • UI design requirements: Describe the design style/layout/icons and colors of the system’s user interface

  • Testing plan and acceptance criteria: Define the system testing plan and acceptance criteria to ensure effective testing after development

  • Implementation constraints and limitations: List any technical limitations such as languages/frameworks and operating systems

  • Time planning and milestones: Clarify the project timeline and milestones to ensure timely completion and expected results

Collaboration Process

  1. Based on your requirements document and further communication to clarify needs, we will provide a quote
  2. Sign a contract
  3. Pay the deposit
  4. Design/development
  5. Testing and acceptance
  6. Pay the balance
  7. Launch and operation
  8. Provide 1-6 months of free maintenance based on project scale (no new requirements changes)
  9. Long-term technical support available on an hourly basis, including requirement updates/application upgrades and maintenance
